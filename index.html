Computerized Eye Testing

At Prabha Eye Hospital, we offer advanced computerized eye testing services under the expert guidance of Dr. Parveen. Our cutting-edge diagnostic tools, including Automated Refraction, Retinal Imaging, and Visual Field Testing, ensure precise and comprehensive eye examinations.

Our specialized tests help in early detection and management of various eye conditions such as glaucoma, macular degeneration, and diabetic retinopathy. Utilizing state-of-the-art technology, we provide quick, accurate, and minimally invasive assessments, enhancing patient comfort and care.

With a commitment to excellence, Prabha Eye Hospital ensures that every patient receives top-notch diagnostic services and personalized care.

Cataract Surgery

Prabha Eye Hospital, led by Dr. Parveen, specializes in advanced cataract surgery techniques. We have successfully performed over 6,000 painless, sutureless phacoemulsification surgeries using the latest technology.

Our services include the use of Multifocal IOLs, Toric IOLs, and EDOF+ IOLs, providing patients with optimal vision correction. Our expertise also extends to complex cases such as non-dilating pupil, post-surgical cataracts, pediatric cataracts, and traumatic cataracts.

We are committed to delivering excellent surgical outcomes with minimal discomfort and quick recovery times, ensuring the best possible care for our patients.

Glaucoma Surgery

Prabha Eye Hospital specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of glaucoma using cutting-edge technology and experienced professionals. We utilize advanced tools like OCT and fundus photography for precise diagnosis, along with tonometry and visual field tests to assess intraocular pressure (IOP) and vision.

Our treatment options range from medications to reduce IOP, to surgical solutions such as trabeculectomy and glaucoma drainage implants for more severe cases. We also provide laser trabeculoplasty, a non-invasive procedure that helps improve fluid drainage in the eye.

Our goal is to protect your vision and enhance your quality of life with personalized glaucoma care.

LASIK Surgery

Welcome to Prabha Eye Hospital, where we offer state-of-the-art LASIK surgery to help you achieve clear vision. Our experienced ophthalmologists and advanced technology ensure precise and safe vision correction for our patients.

What is LASIK?

LASIK (Laser-Assisted In Situ Keratomileusis) is a popular and effective surgical procedure designed to correct common vision problems such as nearsightedness, farsightedness, and astigmatism. The procedure reshapes the cornea to allow light to be properly focused onto the retina, resulting in clearer vision.

Our LASIK Services

  • Minimally Invasive Procedure: LASIK is known for its quick recovery time and minimal discomfort. Most patients experience significant improvement in their vision within a day.
  • Precision and Safety: Our advanced laser technology ensures high precision and safety during the procedure, giving you peace of mind.
  • Personalized Treatment: We customize each LASIK procedure to match the unique characteristics of your eyes, ensuring the best possible outcome for your vision correction needs.

Benefits of LASIK Surgery

  • Quick Recovery: Most patients can return to their normal activities within a day or two after the procedure.
  • Long-lasting Results: Enjoy clear vision without the need for glasses or contact lenses.
  • Improved Quality of Life: Experience the freedom and convenience of life without visual aids.
Lid Surgery

What is Lid Surgery?

Lid surgery, or blepharoplasty, corrects droopy eyelids and removes excess skin, muscle, and fat to improve both vision and appearance.

Benefits

  • Clearer Vision: Fix sagging eyelids that impair sight.
  • Youthful Look: Remove under-eye bags and puffiness.
  • Increased Comfort: Address eyelid issues causing discomfort.
  • Boosted Confidence: Achieve a refreshed, alert appearance.

Why Choose Us?

  • Expert Surgeons: Skilled in the latest techniques.
  • Advanced Technology: State-of-the-art surgical equipment.
  • Personalized Care: From consultation to recovery.

The Procedure

  • Quick and Safe: Outpatient procedure with local anesthesia.
  • Minimal Scarring: Precise incisions along natural eyelid creases.
  • Fast Recovery: Return to normal activities within a week.
